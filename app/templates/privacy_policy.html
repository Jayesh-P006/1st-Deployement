Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the service.

What data we process

  • Account data: username/email you use to access this app.
  • Instagram DM webhook events: message metadata and content that Instagram sends to our webhook endpoint so we can display and (optionally) auto-reply.
  • Operational logs: basic logs to keep the service reliable (errors, request timestamps).

How we use data

  • To receive and process Instagram webhook events.
  • To display DM conversations in the app and enable features like conversation management/auto-replies.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug issues.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as required to operate the service (for example, hosting/database providers) or to comply with legal obligations.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the features you use and operational/security purposes. You can request deletion at any time (see the Data Deletion page).

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
