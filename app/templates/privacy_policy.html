Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this application processes data when you use our service, including when we receive Instagram messaging webhook events.

Data we collect and process

  • App account data: usernames/emails used to log into this app (for team access and administration).
  • Instagram messaging webhook data: when configured, Instagram/Meta may send webhook events to our endpoint containing message data such as sender/recipient IDs, message IDs, timestamps, and message text.
  • Conversation records stored by the app: the app stores DM conversations and messages in its database so they can be displayed in the Conversations view and used for automation features.
  • Automation metadata: settings (for example, whether auto-reply is enabled), and delivery status/error details for outgoing replies.
  • Operational data: logs and diagnostics (for example, errors and request timing) to keep the service reliable and secure.

How we use the data

  • Receive and process webhooks to record incoming Instagram DMs and update conversation history.
  • Display conversations inside the app for your team to view and manage.
  • Send replies through the Instagram Graph API when auto-reply is enabled.
  • Generate suggested/automatic replies using AI only when automation features are enabled/configured in the app.
  • Maintain security and performance (rate limiting, abuse prevention, debugging).

AI processing (if enabled)

If AI reply features are enabled, message text may be processed to generate a reply. We use this processing only to provide messaging automation features and not for unrelated advertising.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app (for example, hosting and database providers) and with Meta/Instagram APIs when sending messages or making API calls.
