User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of data associated with your use of this application.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us a deletion request.
  2. Include enough information to identify the data, such as:
    • Your Instagram username or Instagram User ID (if available)
    • Approximate date/time range of messages
    • Any other identifiers shown in the app (conversation ID)

What we delete

  • Stored DM conversations/messages that were received via webhook events (where applicable).
  • Related metadata stored for conversation display and automation.

Processing time

We will respond and process verified deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.
